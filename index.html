Who We Are

Building trust through design and expertise

At DesignArc Studio, we believe architecture is more than just structures—it’s about creating spaces that inspire, connect, and endure. Since our founding in 2010, we’ve been dedicated to delivering exceptional designs that blend creativity, functionality, and sustainability.
Our team of seasoned architects and designers, led by John Walters and Emma Hudson, combines years of experience with a passion for innovation. From contemporary residences to large-scale commercial developments, we’ve built a reputation for excellence in every project we undertake.
With a client-first approach, we prioritize understanding your vision, needs, and goals, ensuring each project is uniquely tailored to you. Let’s design the future together.
